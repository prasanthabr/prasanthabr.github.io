Martin Fowler has worked years collecting and publishing snippets of original research and information.  
Software is a subject with a short half-life. Content becomes obsolete very quickly.  

Martin Fowler keeps his content relevant for years by
Focussing on writing on the fundamentals of Software architecture and engineering.  

Fads change; but techniques and fundamentals last longer.
