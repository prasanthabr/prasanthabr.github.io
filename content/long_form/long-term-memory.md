Often linked to the larger part of the head behind the prefrontal cortex.  
Has millions of interlinked neurons and patterns that represents ideas and
learning.  
The mental model for the long term memory is a ware house with boxes of ideas or
memory.  
Learnings are represented by patterns that are stored in the long term memory
that act as triggers for this memory.
