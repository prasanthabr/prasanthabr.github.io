## Stages of Design Thinking  
1. CLARIFY - Clarifying an innovation problem.  
    _Based on user observation, research and insights with the aim of finding innovative ways of framing a question_  
2. IDEATE - Generating Ideas and understanding user behaviour  
    _Tools to overcome cognitive fixedness. Making the idea generating exercise useful for addressing the problem at hand_  
3. Developing the Ideas  
    _Prioritizing ideas, improving them based on insights and rapidly prototyping to explore and test critical questions_  
4. Communicating the Value of Innovation 
    _To communicate, build relevance and recruit for the idea and solution_

**Note: Innovation would not be in a sequencial cascade. As the tools of innovation become more familiar, there was be an twists and turns of flow through these different stages are the problem becomes more in focus and less in focus**
