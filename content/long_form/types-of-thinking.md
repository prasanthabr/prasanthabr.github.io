Focussed thinking is when concentrating of a specific subject, consciously
trying to learn or understand.  
The mental model around it is that of a dense set of nodes of ideas.  
Diffused thinking is when the latent memories of the thinking are played in our
minds.  
The mental model is of a diffused set of ideas that are playing back on what was
concentrated on.
