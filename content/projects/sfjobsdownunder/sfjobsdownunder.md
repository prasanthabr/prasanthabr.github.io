Salesforce let go of people I know in Australia & New Zealand. This is part of the 10% global reduction published already. This hit too close, so thought what would help if I was let go. I probably would be quite affected and will not be able to think clearly. It would be helpful if I have to remember one site which gives me all the jobs on offer and relevant material that might help me in my journey. SF Jobs Down Under is a Friday night hack of free services available to put up a site that is performant, can be updated easily and provides basic search or filters.

Inspiration
- Google sheet that lists personal information of laid off employees
- lack of a list specific to New Zealand and Australia region
- Clutter by way of recruiters and recruiting agency adverts
